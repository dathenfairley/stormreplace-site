Hail of this size is known to cause damage to roofing shingles, gutters, seals, and flashing — damage that may not be immediately visible from the ground. Why Getting an Inspection Matters

Hail damage at 0.75 inches can bruise and crack roofing materials in ways that are nearly impossible to detect without a trained eye. Left unaddressed, even minor impact damage can lead to water infiltration, mold, and accelerated shingle deterioration over time.

A timely professional inspection creates documentation of the damage close to the storm date — which may be important if you choose to file a homeowners insurance claim. Insurance coverage outcomes are not guaranteed and depend on your specific policy.
