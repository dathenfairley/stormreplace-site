Why a Prompt Inspection Matters

Hail damage — even from smaller stones — can compromise your roof's protective layer without leaving obvious signs from ground level. Cracked or bruised shingles, damaged flashing, and granule loss can lead to leaks and structural issues over time. Requesting a professional inspection soon after a storm event can help document any damage while it is fresh, which may be useful when filing an insurance claim.
