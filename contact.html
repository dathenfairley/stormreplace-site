Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will a contractor contact me?

After submitting a request during business hours, you can typically expect to hear from a contractor within a few hours.

Is this service really free?

Yes. StormReplace is completely free for homeowners. Contractors pay a fee to receive referrals — you are never charged.

How do I opt out of being contacted?

Reply STOP to any text message, email optout@stormreplace.com, or call (800) 555-1234. Allow up to 10 business days for processing.

I'm a roofing contractor. How do I join your network?

Email contractors@stormreplace.com with your business name, service area, and licensing information.
