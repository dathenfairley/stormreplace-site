Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: January 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how StormReplace ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our website and services. Please read this policy carefully before submitting any information.

1. Who We Are

StormReplace is a contractor referral and matching service that connects homeowners with licensed roofing contractors. We operate as a lead generation service. We are not a roofing contractor and do not perform any roofing services ourselves.

Parent Company: PPC CHAMPION LLC

Business Name: StormReplace
Contact: contact@ppcchampion.com
Address: 5856 Watoga Dr., Hamilton, OH 45011
Phone: (480) 877-0035

2. Information We Collect

When you submit a request through our website, we collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Contact information: Your first and last name, phone number, and email address
  • Property information: Your property address and zip code
  • Project information: Type of damage, property type, and insurance status as you provide them
  • Technical information: IP address, browser type, device type, pages viewed, and referring URL collected automatically when you visit our site
  • Consent records: Records of the consent you provide, including the date, time, IP address, and the exact consent language displayed to you at the time of submission, as documented by our third-party consent verification service (TrustedForm by ActiveProspect)

We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 18.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service delivery: To match you with licensed roofing contractors who serve your geographic area
  • Contractor connection: To share your contact and project information with up to five (5) roofing contractors in our network
  • Communication: To contact you about your request via phone, text, or email as authorized by your consent
  • Compliance: To maintain records of your consent for TCPA compliance purposes
  • Analytics: To understand how our website is used and improve our services
  • Advertising: To operate and optimize our paid advertising campaigns on Google and Meta

4. How We Share Your Information

With Roofing Contractors

When you submit a request, your contact and project information will be shared with up to five (5) roofing contractors in our network who serve your area. These contractors may contact you by phone, text, or email. Each contractor is an independent business with its own privacy practices.

With Service Providers

We work with third-party service providers including ActiveProspect (TrustedForm), Airtable, Twilio, Instantly.ai, Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, Google Ads, and Make.com. These providers are contractually prohibited from using your information for any purpose other than providing services to us.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal process.

5. Your Consent and TCPA

By submitting a request form on our website, you expressly consent to be contacted by StormReplace and the roofing contractors we match you with, by telephone (including cell phone), text message, and email, which may include automated or pre-recorded calls or messages. You are not required to provide this consent as a condition of receiving any goods or services.

You may revoke this consent at any time by replying STOP to any text message, emailing contact@ppcchampion.com, or calling (480) 877-0035.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses essential cookies, Google Analytics cookies, and Google Ads and Meta Pixel advertising cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings. To opt out of Google Analytics, visit Google's opt-out page.

7. Data Retention

  • Contact and project information: Up to 3 years from submission or until you request deletion
  • Consent records (TrustedForm): 5 years, to comply with TCPA statute of limitations
  • Analytics data: Up to 26 months

8. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. California residents have additional rights under CCPA/CPRA — see our Do Not Sell My Info page. To exercise any right, contact us at contact@ppcchampion.com or (480) 877-0035.

9. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
