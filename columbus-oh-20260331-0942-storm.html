Why a Roof Inspection Matters After Hail

Hail damage is deceptive. Even stones under one inch can knock protective granules off shingles, accelerate aging, and create entry points for moisture — all without leaving obvious visible marks from the ground. Wind speeds above 45 mph can lift flashing, loosen ridge caps, and stress seams that were previously sealed.

Having a licensed roofing contractor evaluate your roof after a storm like this is the most practical step you can take to protect your home and understand your options. Inspection costs nothing. Ignoring the damage can cost significantly more.
