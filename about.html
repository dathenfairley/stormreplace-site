About StormReplace

Storm roof replacement, simplified

Important: StormReplace is a contractor referral and matching service. We are not a roofing contractor and do not perform roofing work. All services are provided by independent licensed contractors.

What We Do

StormReplace is a free online service that helps homeowners find licensed, insured roofing contractors in their area. When you submit a request, we review your project information and connect you with up to four roofing contractors from our network who serve your location.

The inspection we facilitate is free and comes with no obligation to hire anyone. You remain in complete control of whether you proceed with any contractor or any work.

How the Process Works

Our Contractor Network

All contractors in the StormReplace network are required to hold a valid roofing contractor license in their state of operation, carry general liability and workers' compensation insurance, and agree to our contractor code of conduct — which includes no-pressure sales practices and free written estimates.

About Insurance and Roof Damage

Many homeowners don't realize that storm or weather-related roof damage may be covered under their homeowner's insurance policy. Whether your damage is covered depends on your specific policy terms, the cause and extent of the damage, your deductible, and your insurer's assessment. We make no representations about whether your claim will be approved or how much you will receive. All insurance decisions are made solely by your insurance provider.

How We Make Money

We operate as a performance-based lead generation business. When we match a homeowner with a contractor, the contractor pays us a referral fee. This fee is paid by the contractor — not by you. Your use of our matching service is always free.
